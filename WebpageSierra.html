Sierra

MacOS Sierra comes with:

  • Siri

  • Icloud drive

  • Picture in Picture

  • Apple File System

  • Apple Photos

  • Apple Pay

High Sierra

High Sierra includes

  • Metal 2
  • HEVC
along with all of the other features that regular sierra offers

Siri

Siri is a personal assistant that interacts with your voice. Siri can do things such as set alarms & timers,
call people, text people, remind you of things, open apps, answer some random questions like how many cups in a litre
along with many more.

Icloud Drive

Icloud stores your photos, videos, documents, music, apps, and more—and keeps them updated across all your devices.
It lets you easily share photos, calendars, locations, and more with friends and family.
You can even use iCloud to help you find your device if you lose it.

Picture in Picture

With Picture in Picture, you can play video in a resizable window that
isn't blocked by other windows, so you can watch while doing other things.

Apple File System

Apple File System is a storage system that comes with most mac os's,
this is the most recent version and it's primary goal is to fix core problems

Apple Photos

Apple Photos lets you store all of your photos on a cloud so you can access them from
all devices and have them backed up

Apple Pay

Use Apple Pay to make purchases in all kinds of apps.
Pay for a ride, a pizza delivery or a new pair of sneakers — with just a touch.

Metal 2

Metal 2 provides near-direct access to the graphics processing unit (GPU),
enabling you to maximize the graphics and compute potential of your apps on iOS, macOS, and tvOS.

High Efficiency Video Coding

HEVC is a higher efficiency video coding so video's take less space.
HEVC is built into iOS 11 and macOS High Sierra,
letting you work with this media in a variety of apps,
including Photos, iMovie, and QuickTime Player.
